Wonders of Africa | Exhibits

1. Kingdom of the Cats

Catch the glimpse of the various felines that call the wilds of Africa home. Whether it be the stubby sand cat of the Sahara, the elusive black panther of the Congo Rainforest, or the King of the Serengeti, the mighty East African lion.

Creatures Shown (but not all): East African Lion, Southeast African Cheetah, African leopard (including the black panther), Sand Cat, African wildcat, Black footed cat, African Golden Cat, Serval, Caracal

2. Birds of the Mother Continent

Gaze at Africa's 2,477 species of birds. Ranging from the mighty ostrich of the Eastern Plains, to the clumsy African penguin to the Southern coast, to arguable the most intelligent avian of all, the African grey Parrot.

Creatures Shown (but not all): Grey crowned crane, Hamercop, Congo pied hornbill, Common Ostrich, Yellow billed stork, Pied kingfisher, Tawny Eagle, African Penguin, Red throated bee eater, Angur buzzard, African grey Parrot, Lovebirds

3. Kenya's Giants

Visit our Gentle Giant exhibit, where visitors can catch a glimpse of Earth's largest living land animals. Check out our Hippo Falls, Rhino Gates, and our most famous attraction, the Elephant Plains.

Creatures Shown (but not all): Common Hippopatumus, Pygmy Hippopatumus, Bush Elephant, Congo Forest Elephant, Northern White Rhinoceros, Black Rhinoceros

4. Mysteries of Madagascar

An island that has been split from the African mainland for 88 million years, Madagascar hosts a bizarre range of curiousity-invoking critters. From millipedes that shoot out acid, to lizards that use color to express themselves, it doesn't get any weirder here at our Mysterious of Madagascar Exhibit.

Creatures Shown (but not all): Mossy leaf-tailed gecko, Day gecko, Panthers Chameleon, Giant Pill millipedes, Ring tailed lemurs Sifakas Mouse lemurs Panther Chameleon Jackson's Chameleon

5. Distant Relatives

Do you know that Chimpanzees share 99% of our DNA. Check out of Distant Relatives Exhibit, where you'll not only meet chimps, but Africa's other non-human primates, including gorillas, mandrills, and bonobos.

Creatures Shown (but not all): Mandrills Western lowland gorillas Mountain gorillas Chimpanzees bonobos Colobus Monkeys Chacma Baboons
